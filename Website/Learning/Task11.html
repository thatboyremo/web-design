How do I report an incident

You can report an incident by clicking on the "report now" button on the homepage then providing details like location, description, and multimedia.

Can I edit or update a report after submission

No, but you can add additional information to the existing report from the incident details page.

How does the system verify incidents

We use crowdsourced verifications from users in the area and automated AI analysis to validate the credibility of reports.
